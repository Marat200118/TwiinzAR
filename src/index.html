About Twiinz.World

Twiinz.world is a contemporary STEAM education tool that focuses on inspiring young people for the Belgian wood, textile and furniture industry.

Within an interactive 3D environment, tweens (10 to 14 year olds) learn and experiment playfully with digital copies (digital twins) of textiles, materials, furniture and other products from Belgian textile, wood and furniture companies.
